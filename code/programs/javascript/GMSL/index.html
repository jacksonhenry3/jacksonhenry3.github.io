Implications of global sea level rise

Global see level rise has the potential to be a major factor in many of the problems the world will face in futuer. The displacement of people living in low level coastal regions has the possiblity to cause great chaos in effected areas. In addition rising sea level may

  • increase storm damage to coastal infrastructure
  • cause more rapid coastal erosion
  • cause shoreline change including the possibility for total loss of protective natural barriers
  • cause saltwater intrusion into aquifers and surface waters
  • cause rising water tables
  • cause changes in tidal prism
